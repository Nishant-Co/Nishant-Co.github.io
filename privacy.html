Privacy Policy

July 16, 2024

Welcome to MotelR, an app designed to help motel owners and managers manage their properties efficiently. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information within the MotelR app.

Information Storage

MotelR does not store any of your data on external servers. All information, including customer data, bookings, and room details, is stored directly on your device. This ensures that your data remains under your control and is not accessible to anyone else.

Data Security

While we strive to ensure the security of your data, please be aware that storing information on your device comes with its own set of risks. We recommend that you regularly back up your data and use secure passwords to protect access to your device.

Data Responsibility

As the user of MotelR, you are responsible for maintaining the security and integrity of your data. MotelR cannot be held liable for any loss, damage, or unauthorized access to your data that may occur as a result of using our app.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or other appropriate means.

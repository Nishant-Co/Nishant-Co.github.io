FAQs

How do I generate a QR code?

To generate a QR code, simply enter the URL in the provided text field and click the "Generate" button. Your QR code will be created instantly and displayed on the screen.

How do I add an image to my QR code?

Click the "Add Photo" button, select an image from your photo library, and it will be embedded into your QR code.

How do I save a QR code to my photo library?

After generating your QR code, click the "Save Image" button to save it directly to your photo library.

How do I share my QR code?

Click the "Share" button to send your QR code through various platforms such as email, social media, and messaging apps.

How do I delete a QR code from my history?

Swipe left on any QR code entry in the History tab to delete it.

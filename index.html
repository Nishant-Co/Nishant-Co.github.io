Welcome to QR Coder.Co, the ultimate app for creating and managing QR codes effortlessly. With QR Coder.Co, you can generate QR codes from URLs, embed images into your QR codes, and save them directly to your photo library. Perfect for personal, educational, or business use, QR Coder.Co ensures your QR codes are high-quality and easily shareable.

Key Features: 1) Generate QR Codes: Quickly generate QR codes from URLs with a simple tap. Just enter the URL in the provided text field and click the "Generate" button to create your QR code instantly. 2)Embed Images: Personalize your QR codes by adding images from your photo library. Click the "Add Photo" button, select an image, and it will be embedded into your QR code seamlessly. 3) Save to Photo Library: Save your generated QR codes directly to your photo library with the "Save Image" button. 4) Share QR Codes: Easily share your QR codes with friends, family, or colleagues via email, social media, or messaging apps by clicking the "Share" button. 5) History Tab: Keep track of your generated QR codes in the History tab. Store up to 50 QR codes along with the associated URLs and the dates they were created. 6) Delete QR Codes: Manage your QR code history by swiping left on any QR code entry in the History tab to delete it. 7) User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a clean, intuitive interface that makes generating and managing QR codes a breeze.
